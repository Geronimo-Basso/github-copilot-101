Mergington High School — Extracurricular Participation Policies
================================================================

1. Each student may sign up for at most three (3) activities per semester.
2. Maximum club size is set per activity and must never be exceeded.
3. Students must use their @mergington.edu email address to register.
4. Once signed up, a student cannot register for the same activity twice.
5. Activity coordinators may add a student to a wait-list when full.
6. Withdrawal requests must be submitted at least 48 hours before the next session.
7. Attendance below 60% in a semester results in automatic removal.
