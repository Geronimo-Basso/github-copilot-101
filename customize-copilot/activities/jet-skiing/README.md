# 🚤 Jet Skiing Adventure

## 📋 Overview

Experience the thrill of jet skiing along the stunning Costa del Sol coastline. This high-energy water sport is perfect for adrenaline seekers who want to explore the Mediterranean Sea at full speed.

## 📍 Details

| Field          | Value                |
| -------------- | -------------------- |
| **Category**   | Water Sports         |
| **Location**   | Costa del Sol, Spain |
| **Duration**   | 1 hour               |
| **Price**      | €75 / person         |
| **Difficulty** | Moderate             |
| **Min. Age**   | 16 years             |

## ✅ What's Included

- Professional instructor and safety briefing
- Jet ski equipment and life jacket
- Guided tour along the coastline
- Photos of your adventure

## 📝 Requirements

- Participants must know how to swim
- Signed liability waiver required
- Sunscreen and swimwear recommended

## ⚠️ Safety Information

- All participants must wear a life jacket at all times
- Follow the instructor's route — do not deviate
- Maximum 2 persons per jet ski
- Activity may be cancelled in case of severe weather
